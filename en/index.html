Producers Association

Sustainable Innovation and Technology

We are an alliance made up of more than 120 coffee families located in the provinces of Jaén and San Ignacio in the Cajamarca region.

With a lot of effort, we have been working since 2020 committed to improving the living conditions of our members families and collaborators of our organization by incorporating technology and innovation for the sustainability of our agriculture and environment.

MISIÓN

Promote a sustainable agriculture that contributes to the well-being of coffee farmers families, care for the environment and promotes the competitiveness of the coffee sector with products that meet high international quality standards.

VISIÓN

We are a competitive coffee organization and leader in the sustainable production of specialty coffees that incorporates technological innovation into its production processes to position ourselves in the international market.
